Bio

In recent years, Tim has utilised his vibrant design skills and extensive coding knowledge to develop aesthetic applications and sleek, responsive websites for a variety of clients.

Some of his current projects have been with reputable organisations such as Queensland Health, along with Education Queensland and Microsoft. Additionally, he has worked closely with local businesses such as asset management firm AML Advisory based in Melbourne, as well as smaller not-for-profit organisations such as QUT Running and QUT EB.

He has experience with a variety of platforms ranging from Java and C# for desktop programs to JQuery and PHP for mobile web experiences.
