About

Hello, my name is Tim. Over the past few years, I've developed extensive knowledge on a wide range of languages, which translates into aesthetic, responsive websites produced for a variety of clients.

I have previously worked with Queensland Health and Education Queensland. I have also worked with smaller not-for-profit organisations such as QUT Running and QUT Exchange (part of the Queensland University of Technology), businesses such as asset management firm AML Advisory and The Logistics Alliance in Melbourne, and well-known local Brisbane Entertainers such as Daryl Murphy and Aaron Maybus.

When I'm not at my computer finding new CSS or JQuery features for my sites, I'm usually out either running, or finding new places to eat great food.
